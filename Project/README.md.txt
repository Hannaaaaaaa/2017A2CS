# This is the Christmas project--BlackJack

#### Introduction
BlackJack is also known as twenty-one, which include two players in our project. Each player draws card in turn. For each turn, the player makes decision according to their previous card: wether to continue or end drawing cards. The player whose sum is closer to 21 wins the game.
